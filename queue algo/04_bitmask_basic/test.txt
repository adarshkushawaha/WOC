Algo 4: The Ultimate O(1) Server Scheduler
This is a High-Performance, Zero-Allocation scheduler designed for massive scale server-side management of mobile workers.

Architecture
Modular Sequence Engine (pkg/dag):
Allows users to define blueprints (e.g., ["Download", "Resize", "Upload"]).
The scheduler executes these steps sequentially without hardcoded logic.
O(1) Bitmask Scheduler (pkg/bitmask):
Uses 64-bit integers (uint64) and CPU instructions (TrailingZeros64) to find the best phone bucket.
Performance: ~20 nanoseconds per scheduling decision.
Scale: Performance is constant regardless of whether you have 10 phones or 10 million.
